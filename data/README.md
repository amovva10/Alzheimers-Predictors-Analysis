# Alzheimer’s Disease Dataset

## Overview
This dataset contains records of 2,143 patients, each with demographic, health, lifestyle, and cognitive assessment information relevant to Alzheimer’s disease. It is intended to support research on the factors associated with Alzheimer’s diagnosis.

## Source
- **Source**: Kaggle
- **URL**: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/rabieelkharoua/alzheimers-disease-dataset 

## Observations
Each row in this dataset represents an individual patient and their associated attributes related to demographic information, lifestyle factors, cognitive assessments, and Alzheimer’s diagnosis.

## Data Dimensions
- **Number of Observations**: 2,143
- **Number of Variables Used**: 17

## Key Variables
| Variable                |  Description                         | Units 
|-------------------------|--------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------|
|`Age`                    | Age of the patient                   | Years                                                     |
|`Gender`                 | Gender of the patient                | 0 = Male, 1 = Female                                      |  
|`Ethnicity`              | Ethnicity of the patient             | 0 = Caucasian, 1 = African American, 2 = Asian, 3 = Other | 
|`Smoking`                | Smoking status.                      | 0 = No, 1 = Yes                                           |
|`PhysicalActivity`       | Weekly physical activity.            | Hours                                                     |
|`FamilyHistoryAlzheimers`| Family history of Alzheimer’s        | 0 = No, 1 = Yes                                           |
|`DietQuality`            | Quality of diet on a scale           | Score from 0 to 10                                        |
|`SleepQuality`           | Quality of sleep on a scale.         | Score from 4 to 10                                        |
|`CholesterolTotal`       | Total cholesterol level.             | mg/dL                                                     |
|`MMSE`                   | Mini-Mental State Examination score. | Score from 0 to 30                                        |
|`ADL`                    | Activities of Daily Living score     |Score from 0 to 10                                         |
|`EducationLevel`         | Highest education level attained.    |0 = None, 1 = High School, 2 = Bachelor’s, 3 = Higher      | 
|`Diagnosis`              | Alzheimer’s diagnosis status.        |0 = No, 1 = Yes                                            |
|`Confusion`              | Presence of confusion                | 0 = No, 1 = Yes                                           |
|`Forgetfulness`          | Presence of forgetfulness            | 0 = No, 1 = Yes                                           |
|`PersonalityChanges`     | Presence of personality changes      | 0 = No, 1 = Yes                                           |
|`MemoryComplaints`       | Presence of memory changes           | 0 = No, 1 = Yes                                           |
| `BMI`                   | Body Mass Index                      | kg/m²                                                     |
